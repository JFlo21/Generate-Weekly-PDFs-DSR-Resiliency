`SUBCONTRACTOR_PPP_SHEET_ID`'s WR# column, (b) the upload-task builder emitting TWO task
entries for `reduced_sub` / `reduced_sub_helper` variants, and (c) the `_upload_one`
worker honoring each task's `target_sheet_id` instead of the global `TARGET_SHEET_ID`.

must apply to BOTH target_maps independently.

either crash or silently swallow `missing_cus` into the wrong variable.
